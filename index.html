Privacy Policy for DopaReset

Effective Date: June 18, 2026

DopaReset ("we," "us," or "our") develops and maintains the DopaReset mobile application. We are committed to protecting the privacy and sensitive emotional data of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle information in accordance with standard mobile application data protection practices and Google Play Developer Policies.

Because your trust is paramount, DopaReset is designed with a "Local First" architecture. This means your most sensitive data never leaves your device.

1. Our Core Privacy Commitment

We do not believe in profiting from your behavioral data. We do not sell, rent, trade, or upload your evaluation responses, your 28-day challenge progress, or your private journal entries to any external servers. All data related to your mental wellness journey is stored encrypted on your local device.

2. Information Collection and Use

A. User-Inputted Sensitive Data (Stored Locally Only)

To provide the core functionality of the 28-Day Reset framework, the app requires you to input information regarding:

  • Your initial behavioral evaluation responses.
  • Your daily completion status of wellness tasks (e.g., Cold Shower, Deep Reading).
  • Your private entries in the "誠實日記" (Honesty Journal).

Handling: This information is processed exclusively on your device. We cannot access, read, or aggregate this data.

B. Non-Personal App State (Stored Locally)

The app stores non-identifiable configuration data necessary for operation, such as your language preference (Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, English, Español) and the current day (e.g., Day 1/28) of your reset protocol.

3. Permissions and Device Access

DopaReset may require minimal permissions for essential functionality:

  • Storage Access: Necessary to securely write and read your encrypted journal database and progress settings locally on your device, ensuring data persistence across app restarts.
  • Network Access (Minimal): Minimal access may be used for standard API licensing checks or fetching language pack updates (if not bundled). No personal data is transmitted during these checks.

4. Data Security

While we do not host your data, we recommend you maintain standard device security practices (e.g., device passwords/biometrics) to ensure your locally stored private journals remain secure from unauthorized physical access to your device.

5. Children’s Privacy

Given the behavioral nature of the Dopamine Reset framework, DopaReset is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes.
